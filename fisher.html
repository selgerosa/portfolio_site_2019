Sherri Fisher is an executive coach, a learning specialist, and a public speaker. Before releasing her new self-help book, Unleash Your Epic Self, she needed a website that would communicate her experience and technique, while also being fun and engaging. Inspired by the bright, colorful illustrations found in her new book, I redesigned and coded her new fully responsive site using HTML/CSS/Javascript. The site redesign included a focus on search engine optimization and accessibility. I also designed her logo to have the same austere playfulness.
