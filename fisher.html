Website Rebranding

Sherri Fisher - Coach and Public Speaker

Sherri Fisher is an executive coach, learning specialist, and a public speaker. Before releasing her new self-help book Unleash Your Epic Self, she needed a fun, engaging website that communicated her experience and technique. Inspired by the bright, colorful illustrations found in the book, I redesigned and coded her new site using HTML/CSS/Javascript. For the redesign, I focused on full responsiveness, accessibility, and exceptional search engine optimization. I also designed her a logo that reflected her crisp, playful brand.
