Brand Consulting and Single Page Site

World Damanation - Voice Actor

Nicole Dama is a dynamic voice actor who needed a friendly, accessible website for her new business that spoke to her visual tastes. I partnered with copywriter Adele Costa to rebrand her website. In our concepting discussions, Nicole gravitated towards a clean but colorful look and feel, so I balanced a bright aesthetic with an intuitive, modular design to achieve this balance and to reflect her personality. The site is fully responsive and is also designed to easily feature a number of videos and audio recordings, so that Nicole's work is always front and center. Finally, I curated several old-timey, eye-catching sound wave and microphone icons to add visual interest and tie the site's form and function together.
