✨Star Prints✨

I am making handmade, unique star prints! There are two different designs to choose from: a light and super cute skyscape with customizable watercolor background or an intense and complex black and white spacescape. When displayed together, they create beautiful and original night skies!

These prints also come in pin form!

Purchase Info

- $5-15 sliding scale + shipping; I know times are tough for everyone right now, so just let me know what you think is fair to pay.

- 5x7 inches

- Waterproof linocut block print and watercolor on Arches watercolor paper.

- As these custom prints are made to order, please allow for up to a week for production.

- I am currently only able to ship in the US.

* Each piece is hand printed, so please be aware that no two prints will be exacly alike. *
