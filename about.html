Hello there, I'm Selina Gerosa. I'm a graphic and web designer based in NY. I have always loved problem solving, and to me, that's what design is all about. It's the system that helps us solve problems in innovative ways.

I recently recieved a BFA in graphic design from Boston University, but my creative journey began way before that. In my middle school art class, one of our projects was to regularly use a sketchbook. I began keeping one with me wherever I went (I still do, in fact, you can see I've been making @sketcharoony). I found my passion for graphic design in high school after taking an AP art class.

Nowadays, I spend most of my time designing websites, running social media campaigns, and generating brand identities. In my spare time, I read academic papers about internet culture, watch niche cartoons, and make mini websites @https://selinas-site-experiments.netlify.com.
